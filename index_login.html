Frequently Asked Questions

With Personalify, you can include various types of content in your portfolio, such as images, videos, text, links to external websites or projects, and documents (PDFs, resumes, etc.). You have the flexibility to showcase your work in the format that best suits your needs.

By default, your portfolio is private, and only you can view it. However, you have the option to make your portfolio public and share it with others by generating a unique URL or embedding it on your website or social media profiles.

Personalify offers both free and premium subscription plans. The free plan includes basic features and allows you to create a portfolio at no cost. The premium plans offer additional features and customization options for a monthly or annual subscription fee.
